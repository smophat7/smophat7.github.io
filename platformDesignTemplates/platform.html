All Templates

Sometimes you just need someone else to do it, and that's why we are here. Just pick a template to get started and you can worry about the details later. If it's missing something you need, you are always free to add more elements.

People

Customer Information

Make sure your team knows your customers by name. And by phone number. And email. And credit card. It's not creepy though, just good business.

Client Relations

We know client relationships are vital, so be sure to stay on top of it. Contact info, next meetings, deal history, their kids' names. The works.

Employee Manager

Even with just a few workers it can be hard to keep tabs on them all. Here you can see their wages, position, hours, schedule, benefits, and more.

Personal Info

Sometimes coworkers can feel like family. At least that's the goal, isn't it? Make that happen by remembering each other's birthdays, favorite food, etc.

Financial

Sales Tracker

If you're a part of a business, you hopefully sell stuff. See how much of what is selling when to who for how much and (maybe) even why.

Sales Forecast

So you know where your sales are now, but you really want to see where they're headed. Factor in previous patterns, personnel turnover, all of it.

Expense Report

Nobody likes bills but it has to be done. For some reason, suppliers demand payment. This will help not let anything slip through the cracks.

Wage Brackets

Someone ask for a raise? Use this to see what that means for the department or team and how that would affect your bottom line. Fingers crossed!

Calendar and Time

Shift Scheduler

You can try to memorize your schedule, but please don't. Break it down by day, week, month, department, employee, overtime, or part time. You get it.

Incoming Shipments

Dream big, but make plans to get there. See the whole week, month, or quarter at once. Heck, you can even see the whole third if you want.

Meeting Calendar

Closely integrated with Google Calendar, Outlook, and other services. Use this for simple, easy scheduling with your coworkers and clients.

Trip Planner

Whether for work or taking time off, people may go places frequently. Organize your plans and tickets. You can even set up message forwarding.
